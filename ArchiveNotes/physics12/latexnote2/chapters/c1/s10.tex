\section{Tension, compression and Pulleys}
\textbf{\textit{Internal Forces}} are Newton's third-law pairs of forces that act within an object or a system of objects
that are in contact with each other. \\

According to Newton's third law, when we analyze the system, the sum of the forces equals 0. 
Thus, these forces will have no impact on the net force acting on the system and will have 
\textbf{\textit{no effect on the system's acceleration}}

\subsection{Tension: T}
If one object is pulling another object, then you are dealing with \textbf{tension}.

\subsection{Compression: C}
If one object is pushing another object, then you are dealing with \textbf{compression}.
